Delete Account – Astro Lark

You can delete your account directly inside the Astro Lark app.

How to delete your account

  1. Log in to the app
  2. Go to your profile page
  3. Tap "Delete Account"

Data deletion

When you delete your account, all associated personal data including your email, username, birth data, and generated astrology data are permanently deleted.
